SET A Foundation - Basic Concepts

Define force and give SI unit.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Force is a push/pull; SI unit is newton (N).

State Ohm’s law in words.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Current is directly proportional to voltage at constant temperature.

Write relation between voltage, current, resistance.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: V = IR.

Differentiate series and parallel circuits.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Series: one path; parallel: multiple paths.

What is wavelength and frequency?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Wavelength is distance between wave crests; frequency is waves per second.

State one property of sound waves.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Sound is a longitudinal mechanical wave.

Define reflection of light.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Bouncing back of light from a surface.

What is magnetism?
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Force related to magnetic fields and magnetic materials.

Name two renewable energy sources.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Solar and wind.

Why are conductors used in wires?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: They allow electric current to flow easily due to low resistance.

SET C Application - Context and Reasoning

Design an experiment to compare brightness in series vs parallel bulbs.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Keep same battery and bulbs; vary circuit arrangement only.

What variable would you measure for brightness objectively?
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Current/voltage at each bulb or light intensity using sensor.

Data: bulb current in series=0.20 A, parallel branch=0.35 A. Which brighter?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Parallel branch likely brighter due to higher current.

Plan a wave demo to measure frequency from oscillations.

SET D Challenge - Complex and Extended

Create a model to estimate monthly energy use from 3 appliances.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Compute kWh = power(kW) x hours used and sum.

A 100 W bulb runs 5 h/day for 30 days. Energy in kWh?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: 0.1 x 5 x 30 = 15 kWh.

If electricity cost is ₹8/kWh, find cost for above bulb.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Cost = 15 x 8 = ₹120.

Explain one reason LEDs save more energy than incandescent bulbs.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Higher efficiency: more electrical energy converted to light, less heat loss.

How does insulation reduce energy waste in buildings?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: It reduces heat transfer, lowering heating/cooling demand.
