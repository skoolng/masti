Write three examples of proper fractions. Easy
Show Answer

Any fractions where numerator < denominator, e.g. 1/2, 3/7, 5/8.

Show Answer

Show Answer

Numerator = 7; Denominator = 12.

Show Answer

A fraction has denominator 8. What values can the numerator take so the fraction is proper? Medium

Numerator can be 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, or 7 (i.e. any positive integer less than 8).

How many improper fractions have denominator 5 and numerator between 5 and 10 inclusive? List them. Hard

5/5, 6/5, 7/5, 8/5, 9/5, 10/5 — that is 6 fractions.

Fractions on the Number Line

Set A Locating Proper Fractions

On a number line from 0 to 1, how many equal parts are needed to show 1/4? Mark 1/4, 2/4, and 3/4. Easy
Show Answer

4 equal parts. 1/4 is at the 1st mark, 2/4 (= 1/2) at the 2nd mark, 3/4 at the 3rd mark.

Where does 3/5 lie on a number line from 0 to 1 divided into 5 equal parts? Easy
Show Answer

At the 3rd mark out of 5 equal divisions — past the midpoint, closer to 1.

Which fraction is exactly at the midpoint between 0 and 1? Easy
Show Answer

1/2.

Mark 1/3 and 2/3 on a number line from 0 to 1. Which is closer to 1? Easy
Show Answer

2/3 is closer to 1 (it is 1/3 away from 1, while 1/3 is 2/3 away).

A number line from 0 to 1 is divided into 8 equal parts. Which mark represents 5/8? Easy
Show Answer

The 5th mark from 0.

What fraction does the midpoint between 1/4 and 3/4 represent? Medium
Show Answer

Midpoint = (1/4 + 3/4)/2 = 1/2.

On a number line, which fraction is halfway between 0 and 1/2? Medium
Show Answer

1/4.

List all fractions with denominator 6 between 0 and 1 (exclusive). Medium
Show Answer

1/6, 2/6, 3/6, 4/6, 5/6.

Between which two consecutive fractions with denominator 5 does 7/10 lie? Hard
Show Answer

7/10 = 3.5/5, so it lies between 3/5 and 4/5.

A number line shows marks at every 1/12. How many marks are between 1/3 and 2/3 (not including the endpoints)? Hard
Show Answer

1/3 = 4/12, 2/3 = 8/12. Marks between: 5/12, 6/12, 7/12 → 3 marks.

Show Answer

On a number line divided into thirds, which mark represents 11/3? Medium
Show Answer

Which is further right on the number line: 9/4 or 7/3? Medium

How far apart are 3/2 and 5/2 on the number line? Medium

Find the fraction exactly midway between 7/4 and 11/4. Hard
Show Answer

How many halves (fractions with denominator 2) lie strictly between 1 and 4 on the number line? List them. Hard
Show Answer

3/2, 4/2, 5/2, 6/2, 7/2 → 5 halves (4/2 = 2 and 6/2 = 3 are whole but still halves).

Set D Mixed Practice — Number Line Problems

A number line goes from 0 to 2. If it is divided into 8 equal parts, what is the value of each part? Easy
Show Answer

Each part = 2/8 = 1/4.

On a number line 0–3 divided into thirds, what is the value of the 7th mark from 0? Easy
Show Answer

7th mark = 7/3 = 2⅓.

The 5th mark on a number line represents 5/6. What number does the 10th mark represent? Medium
Show Answer

Each mark = 1/6. 10th mark = 10/6 = 5/3 = 1⅔.

The distance between 0 and 3/5 is divided into 3 equal parts. What fraction does each small part represent? Medium
Show Answer

(3/5) ÷ 3 = 3/5 × 1/3 = 1/5. Each small part = 1/5.

On a number line, P is at 7/4 and Q is at 11/4. What is the distance PQ? Medium
Show Answer

11/4 − 7/4 = 4/4 = 1.

Mark A is at 1/3 and mark B is at 5/6. What fraction is exactly in the middle of A and B? Medium
Show Answer

(1/3 + 5/6)/2 = (2/6 + 5/6)/2 = (7/6)/2 = 7/12.

A worm crawls from 0 to 4/5 in 4 equal steps. Where is it after each step? Medium
Show Answer

Each step = 4/5 ÷ 4 = 1/5. Positions: 1/5, 2/5, 3/5, 4/5.

Three points are equally spaced between 1 and 2 on the number line. What are their values? Hard
Show Answer

Interval = 1. With 3 interior points, spacing = 1/4. Points: 5/4, 6/4 (=3/2), 7/4.

A number line shows: 0, ___, 2/9, ___, 4/9, ___, 6/9. What are the missing values? Hard
Show Answer

Missing values: 1/9, 3/9, 5/9.

If 3/8 and 7/8 are both marked, and the number line between them is split into 4 equal parts, what are the 3 interior points? Hard
Show Answer

Gap = 7/8 − 3/8 = 4/8 = 1/2. Spacing = 1/8. Interior points: 4/8, 5/8, 6/8.
