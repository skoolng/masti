State the classical definition of probability. What conditions must be met for it to apply?Easy

Easy

Show Answer

n(S) = 2, n(E) = 1

Easy

Show Answer

Easy

Show Answer

Even = {2,4,6}, n(E) = 3, n(S) = 6

Easy
Cards numbered 1–20 are placed in a box. Find P(drawing a multiple of 4).
Show Answer

Multiples of 4 up to 20: {4,8,12,16,20} → n(E) = 5, n(S) = 20

Easy
A bag has 3 white, 5 black balls. Find P(black ball).
Show Answer

n(S) = 8, n(black) = 5
P(black) = 5/8

Easy
Two coins are tossed. Find P(both heads).
Show Answer

S = {HH, HT, TH, TT}, n(S) = 4
E = {HH}, n(E) = 1 → P = 1/4

Easy
A die is rolled. Find P(number greater than 2).
Show Answer

E = {3,4,5,6}, n(E) = 4, n(S) = 6
P = 4/6 = 2/3

Easy
A card is drawn from a standard deck of 52 cards. Find P(drawing an ace).
Show Answer

n(aces) = 4, n(S) = 52
P(ace) = 4/52 = 1/13
