SET A Foundation - Basic Concepts

Differentiate weather and climate.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Weather is short-term atmospheric condition; climate is long-term pattern.

Name layers of Earth from inside out.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Inner core, outer core, mantle, crust.

What is erosion?
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Wearing away of land by water, wind, ice, or gravity.

Name one greenhouse gas.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Carbon dioxide (also methane, nitrous oxide, water vapor).

Define renewable and non-renewable resources.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Renewable replenish naturally; non-renewable are finite on human timescales.

What is ecosystem?
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: A community of organisms interacting with each other and environment.

State one cause of ocean pollution.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Plastic waste, oil spills, sewage discharge, chemicals.

Why is biodiversity linked to ecosystem resilience?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Higher diversity supports stability under disturbances.

What is carbon footprint?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Total greenhouse gas emissions caused by an activity/person.

Give one personal action to reduce environmental impact.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Use public transport, reduce waste, conserve energy/water.

SET B Core Practice - Skill Building

A city’s avg temp rose from 28°C to 29.4°C. Find increase %.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Increase = 1.4/28 x100 = 5%.

Classify as biotic/abiotic: sunlight, algae, fish, pH.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Biotic: algae, fish. Abiotic: sunlight, pH.

Why can deforestation increase flood risk?
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Less vegetation means less water absorption and more runoff.

State one impact of soil erosion on agriculture.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Reduced fertile topsoil lowers crop productivity.

How does acid rain affect buildings/ecosystems?
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Corrodes structures and harms aquatic/soil systems.

Name one method to monitor air quality.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: PM2.5/PM10 sensors, NOx/SO2 monitoring stations.

What is food web compared with food chain?
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Food web is interconnected food chains showing multiple pathways.

Suggest one indicator species and why useful.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Lichens indicate air quality; sensitive to pollutants.

How can urban heat islands form?
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Concrete/asphalt absorb and retain heat; reduced vegetation.

Define sustainable development in one sentence.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Meeting present needs without compromising future generations.

SET C Application - Context and Reasoning

Plan a school waste audit for one week.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Categorize waste (paper/plastic/organic), record daily mass/volume.

If plastic waste was 18, 16, 20, 14, 12 kg over five days, find mean.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Mean = 16 kg/day.

Calculate % reduction from day1 (18 kg) to day5 (12 kg).
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Reduction = 6/18 x100 = 33.3%.

Suggest one reason for variation in daily waste data.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Different attendance/events/canteen menu.

Propose one intervention to reduce plastic waste.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Reusable bottles/containers and refill stations.

How would you test intervention effectiveness?
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Compare baseline vs post-intervention mean waste over equal duration.

Write one limitation of short audit period.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: May not represent long-term patterns.

State one ethical/safety issue during waste audit.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Use gloves and proper handling to avoid contamination/injury.

Create a graph type suitable for daily waste trend.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Line graph for trend over time.

Write CER from waste data and intervention proposal.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Claim reduction possible, evidence from trend, reasoning via behavior change/system design.
