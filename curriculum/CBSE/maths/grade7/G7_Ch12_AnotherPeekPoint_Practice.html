Explain why 0.999... (repeating) equals 1.
Hard
Answer

Answer: Let x = 0.999... Then 10x = 9.999... So 9x = 9 → x = 1.

Method: Algebraic Proof
Step 1: Let x = 0.9̄ = 0.999...

Key Rule: 0.9̄ = 1; repeating decimals are exact fractions.
