SET A Foundation - Key Ideas

State what a tangent is.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: A line that touches a circle at exactly one point.

State one theorem about a radius and a tangent.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: A radius is perpendicular to a tangent at the point of contact.

If an angle at the centre is 100 deg, what is the angle at the circumference standing on the same arc?
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: 50 deg

State one property of a cyclic quadrilateral.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Opposite angles sum to 180 deg.

Explain the difference between a chord and a secant.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: A chord is a segment with endpoints on the circle; a secant is a full line cutting the circle twice.
