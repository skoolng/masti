What is Perimeter? (Concept — Distance Around a Shape)

Set A Basic Concept and Simple Counting

What does "perimeter" mean?
Easy
Answer

Answer: Perimeter is the total distance around the outside boundary of a shape.

Method: Definition
Step 1: Recall: perimeter = total distance around the outside edge of a shape
Step 2: It is measured in units of length: mm, cm, m, km
Step 3: The boundary forms a closed path around the shape
Key Rule: Perimeter = sum of all side lengths of a closed shape
