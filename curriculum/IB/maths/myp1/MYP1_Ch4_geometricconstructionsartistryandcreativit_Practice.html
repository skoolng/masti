SET A Foundation - Basic Concepts

Find perimeter of rectangle 12 cm by 7 cm.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Perimeter = 2(12+7) = 38 cm.

Find area of rectangle 15 cm by 9 cm.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Area = 15x9 = 135 cm^2.

Find area of triangle with base 10 cm and height 8 cm.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Area = 1/2 x 10 x 8 = 40 cm^2.

Find volume of cuboid 8 cm x 5 cm x 4 cm.
Easy
Show Answer

Answer: Volume = 160 cm^3.

A circle has radius 7 cm. Find circumference (pi=22/7).
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Circumference = 2pi r = 44 cm.

Find area of same circle.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Area = pi r^2 = 154 cm^2.

Find diagonal of rectangle 9 cm by 12 cm.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Diagonal = sqrt(9^2+12^2)=15 cm.

In an isosceles triangle, equal sides 10 cm, base 12 cm, find height.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: Half-base 6; height=sqrt(10^2-6^2)=8 cm.

A cone has r=3 cm, h=4 cm. Find slant height.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: l = sqrt(3^2+4^2)=5 cm.

Find TSA of cuboid 6 cm x 4 cm x 3 cm.
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: TSA=2(lw+lh+wh)=108 cm^2.

SET D Challenge - Complex and Extended

A frustum has radii 6 cm and 3 cm, height 8 cm. Volume?
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: V=(1/3)pi h(R^2+r^2+Rr)=168pi cm^3.

Prove angle in semicircle is 90° (state theorem).
Hard
Show Answer

Answer: By Thales theorem, angle subtended by diameter at circumference is right angle.

A cone and cylinder have same r=4 cm and h=9 cm. Compare volumes.
Medium
Show Answer

Answer: Cone volume is one-third of cylinder volume.

A cube side 6 cm is melted into smaller cubes side 2 cm. Number of cubes?
