            How to Play Algbrox Runner

            7+3
            Math Gates: A problem appears above. Three gates show possible answers. Move your runner into the lane with the correct answer before you reach the gate.
            JUMP
            Orange obstacles: Press ↑ / Space or tap Jump while in that lane — or just switch to a different lane to avoid it.
            SLIDE
            Blue obstacles: Press ↓ / S or tap Slide while in that lane — or just switch to a different lane to avoid it.

            Move lanes: ← → arrow keys, A D, or tap the Left / Right buttons below the canvas. The highlighted answer button shows your current lane.
